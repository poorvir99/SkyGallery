Hello,Welcome to my Gallery.This is a selection of what I consider to be some of my best photos, shot with my phone anytime the sky caught my eye.I'm someone who adores the sky in all its forms and who appreciates in viewing the sunsets,stars,clouds,moon. Opacarophile, Astrophile, Nephophile, and Selenophile are the terms for each of them. You can see my Instagram page, where I sometimes share pictures of the sky.

An opacarophile is a lover of sunsets. It is composed of "opacare" which is Latin for sunset (or dusk), and 'phile' which is Greek for love. Didn't know there was a word for we lovers of setting suns. I'm definitely one of them.

An astrophile is a person who loves astronomy or constantly gazes at the stars.

Nephophilia is a person who loves clouds; love of clouds; fondness or obsession of clouds. Words to describe someone, Clouds, Person.

Selenophile : a person who loves the moon.
